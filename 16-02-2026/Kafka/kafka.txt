Instead of apps talking directly,
they send messages through Kafka.

Kafka stores the message safely,
and other services consume it.

User places order
        ↓
 Order Service (Producer)
        ↓
        Kafka
        ↓
 ┌──────────────┬──────────────┬──────────────┐
 Payment Service Inventory Service Email Service
   (Consumer)        (Consumer)      (Consumer)


Each service consumes the same event independently.

No direct coupling.


Think of Kafka like:
A Post Office
Producer = Person sending package
Kafka = Post Office storing & distributing
Consumer = Person receiving package


Kafka is a distributed message pipeline that allows multiple services to communicate reliably and at large scale.
